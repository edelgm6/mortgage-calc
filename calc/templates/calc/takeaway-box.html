Investment returns (IRR):

Your peak annualized return is % if you sell in year . The long run annualized return of the S&P500 is ~7-9%.

Value Levers:

Shows the effect on returns from different parts of the investment. For example, if your year 30 IRR is 5% and your year 30 Tax Shield value lever is 1%, then your year 30 IRR would be 4% without the Tax Shield.

Assumptions:

-Appreciation: 0% yearly appreciation
-Mortgage: Payment in cash up front
-Avoided rent: No alternative rent
-Tax shield: No tax breaks for interest or state and local taxes
