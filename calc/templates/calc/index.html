Mortgage ROI Calculator

There are a lot of mortgage calculators out there, but they cluster around very simple questions -- what will my mortgage payment be, how expensive of a house can I afford, when is it cheaper to rent or buy. But there's another more important question: Is buying a house the best investment of my money, or might I be better off renting and putting it in the stock market? The Mortgage ROI calculator does just that. Enter your assumptions and it takes care of the rest.
